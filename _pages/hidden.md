# 15-Minute Lower-Body Add-On for Runners  

| Block | Time | What & Why |
|-------|------|------------|
| **1. Activation** | ≈ 3 min | Mini-band monster walk 20 m out / 20 m back  <br> Standing calf skips × 30 s  <br> Dynamic hip-flexor stretch × 30 s / leg |
| **2. Strength Superset** | ≈ 8 min | **Pick one pairing per session** (40 s work / 20 s swap, 2 rounds). |
| **3. Power Finisher** | ≈ 4 min | Pogo hops *or* A-skips: 3 × 10 s, 20 s rest. |
| **4. 1-Min Mobility Cool-down** | — | Kneeling hip-flexor stretch -> calf wall stretch -> prone quad stretch (20 s each). |

---

## Strength options

| Session | A1 – **Load/Quad-dominant** | A2 – **Posterior/Core-dominant** |
|---------|-----------------------------|----------------------------------|
| **A** | **Goblet squat** – 6-8 reps (DB/KB, keep elbows under) | Single-leg RDL – 8 reps / leg |
| **B** | **Bulgarian split squat** – 6-8 reps / leg (DBs) | Nordic ham curl – 5 slow reps |
| **C** | **Front-rack step-up** 8 reps / leg | Lateral band walk – 12 steps each way |
| **D** | **Front squat cluster** – 2 × 3 reps @ ~80 % 1 RM (15 s between triples) | Glute-bridge march – 10 reps / leg |
